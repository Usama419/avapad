WHAT IS AVAPAD?
Avapad is a community oriented decentralized launchpad that
high potential projects running on Avalanche Network.

WHY CHOOSE US?
Avapad is built up for need of community trust and togetherness.
Wide range tier system and two token mechanism have been
established to cover all investors and community members. Avapad
provides a fair and decentralized IDO participation process, and while
ensuring this, it aims to achieve a balance of gains for both the
investor and the entrepreneur. When choosing projects, it pays
attention to the following criteria:
- Project owners resumes and qualifications
- Project stakeholders' and investors' references
- Roadmap of the project
- Project documents and outputs
- Tokenomics
- Activity of social media pages and website quality
- Adequacy to provide the required liquidity
Avapad will only support projects that meet these criteria throughout their
roadmaps. At the same time, Avapad charges low fees on all transactions
through the Avalanche Network. In this way, it provides convenience to
investors in terms of transaction mobility.
